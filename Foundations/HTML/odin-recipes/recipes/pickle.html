Pickles

Description

Pickles are crunchy, sour, and delicious

Pickles are a questionable source of probiotics

Ingredients

  • cucumber
  • 1 liter water
  • 200 grams salt
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 kg dill

steps

  1. combine ingredients
  2. place in cupboard and forget about for next 3 months
  3. once pickles look sufficiently musty, refrigerate
  4. enjoy if able
